Healthy Relationships

Warm up your mind, then play a quick game to learn how to spot the signs of a healthy — and unhealthy — relationship. 💚

Play · learn · reflect Private & judgment-free Real-life scenarios

Choose an activity

Start with the warm-up, or jump straight into the game.

Before We Begin

A few quick questions to get your head in the room before class — mostly just tap to answer.

Spot the Flag

Thirteen real relationship behaviors. Decide: green flag, or red flag — and find out why it matters.

Myth or Fact

Bust common myths about relationships, consent, and staying safe — quick true-or-false rounds.

Boundary Sort

Sort everyday situations by how they sit with you. No wrong answers — your boundaries are yours.

After Class

Take a minute to reflect — what you learned, how confident you feel, and any questions. Save a copy or submit to your teacher.
